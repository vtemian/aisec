Q: What is Outclan?
R: A new type of real-time, location-based socially integrated game.

Q: What do I need to play?
R: So far, you only need a web browser, and some Android-based, GPS-enabled mobile phone.

Q: Why do I need both of those?
R: It's simple. You get on the website, sign up, and download our app. Then you can start using it to checkin to any venue you go to. You use the app to checkin and the website to manage everything else.

Q: So, is it only on Android?
R: For now. We plan to expand to a lot of other systems, mobile, and PC-based.

Q: Why should I play?
R: Why not? It's fun, addictive in a positive manner, most importantly, gets you out of the house. Do you want a game that replaces your actual life, giving you a faint impression of fun, or do you want a game that can seamlessly integrate with your life? We offer the second.

Q: I'm still confused. What can I manage on the website?
R: Territories, clans, you name it. Take the tour and learn more.
